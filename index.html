I am a first-year PhD student in the Economics Department at UMass Amherst. Prior to coming to UMass, I earned a BA in Mathematics and Economics and an MS in Systems Engineering from Cornell University.

My research interests are diverse, but lie broadly in the realm of macroeconomics and international economics. I am particularly interested in studying the processes which give rise to wealth and income inequality in capitalist economies.

On this site you may find my CV, as well as more in-depth summaries of my research and teaching experience. For inquiries of any sort, please reach out to me using any of the contact info links on the left!
